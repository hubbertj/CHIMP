About

CHIMP (COVID-19 Heat Map Application) is a interactive program used for providing the latest news for COVID-19. Orginal created as Regis University senior capstone project. This sites intention is to filter through all the misinformation about COVID-19 and help people get to the true. CHIMP also offer live data for COVID-19 status in your area. Stay in the known and Stay safe.
